What I'm About

I love creating digital art. 2D artwork, 2D animations, 3D animations, all sorts of videos, and video games. I love playing video games just as much as I love making them.
I also enjoy playing Tennis and Dungeon and Dragons with my friends. I enjoy creating homebrew D&D content and campaigns as well. Physcial projects are something I
enjoy working on. I've made props from video games, anime, movies, working electrical gadgets, and modifications to make things I own more custom. Currently I'm
working on a recreation of a real life Samurai Edge from Resident Evil. I'm deep into studying Japanese at the moment, as I hope to one day move to Tokyo soon.

My Food Haunts

  • I frequent Michi Ramen quite often. Most of the time with friends, but every now and then I'll go on my own and just sit at the bar. Sometimes I study Japanese, sometimes
    I'm working on a school project.

  • Doc's Sandwiches is an awesome sandwhich shop I often swing by. They have a wide selection of both hot and cold sandwiches, and I've never once been dissapointed.

  • If you can, make sure to give M-80 Chicken a try if you ever get the chance. M-80 is one of Missoula's mobile van resturants. They have, by far, the best chicken sandwhich
    I've ever had. Be prepared for some spice though.
